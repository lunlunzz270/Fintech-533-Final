Strategy Methodology

Entry Logic

The strategy employs a Sector-Neutral framework to eliminate macro-sector biases and focus on idiosyncratic alpha.

  • Universe: S&P 500 constituents.
  • Factor Library: IC-weighted combination of Momentum (12m and 3m), Proximity to 52-week High, and Trend Quality.
  • Optimization: 3-year rolling walk-forward training window to adapt to regime shifts.
  • Selection: Daily rebalancing into the top 10% of stocks within each sector.

Risk Management

Strict risk controls are embedded to preserve capital and ensure strategy longevity.

  • Exit Mechanism: Daily portfolio rotation ensures exposure is always aligned with the freshest signals.
  • Stop-Loss: A hard 3.0% protective stop-loss triggers an immediate exit if intra-day volatility breaches the threshold.
  • Fate Tracking: Every trade is classified as Success, Stop-Loss, or Timeout for rigorous post-trade analysis.

Monitoring & Robustness

How do we know if it’s working? We monitor the IC Stability. As long as the realized signal IC remains within 1 standard deviation of our historical mean (~0.05) and the rolling success rate stays above 48%, the model is considered healthy.

When do we stop? A drawdown exceeding 24.5% (1.5x historical max) or two consecutive 6-month folds with negative excess returns will trigger an automatic strategy halt and factor re-evaluation.
